Services we offer

Gow is a program that aims to teach middle school students who are living in an unsafe environment and are exposed to crimes and boredom. It provides vocational training in the field of technology to empower them with skills and values now and in the future.

What is GOW?
Gow is a program that aims to teach Israeli and Palestinian middle school students who are living in an unsafe environment and are exposed to crimes on a daily basis, and as a result, they are deprived of stimulating learning opportunities. It provides vocational training in the field of technology to empower them with skills and values now and in the future.
