About GOW

Gow is a program that aims to teach middle school students who are living in an unsafe environment ,it provide vocational training in the field of technology to empower them with skills and values that would help them now and in the future

GOW provides kids with:

  • Useful vocational knowledge- basic skills and knowledge of a cellular technician. Alumnis can actually be employed in cellular labs.
  • Intrentaiment- we give the participants an outlet for after school hours. We know that a busy teenager is much less likely to make trouble, compared to a board teenager.
  • Individuality and self confidence - in adulessens all teenagers are rebuilding their self confidence, and desperately looking for empowering sources. Owning useful skeels and being a part of a big group can mean a lot when it comes to self confidence and self esteem.
  • Fun and joy - the revenues will be invested in fun activities for the program participants (as a pool day, movie night, a trip, etc…). In that way the teenagers will be rewarded for their efforts.
