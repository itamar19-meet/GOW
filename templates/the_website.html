About GOW

GOW provides a free vocational training program to Israeli and Palestinian middle school students with high drop out rstes to empower them to take control of their own future.

GOW provides:

  • Useful vocational knowledge - The skills and knowledge to repair desktop computers and various electronic devices. Alumni of the program will have employable skills in the electronic industry.
  • Entertainment -Having fun, while developing skills, is also a priority of ours. Beach trips, Escape rooms and other fun activities will be provided as a reward for hard work.
  • Self development -Middle school is the time in which youth can form their self-image and better understand their potential. We will provide them the platform they need to express their abilities

Mission

Our mission is to empower and support middle school students by providing them with useful skills, a fun and safe environment and a way to express their individuality.

Vision

We envision a reality where all teenagers have a chance to grow up in a healthy environment, and to become productive and proud adults.
